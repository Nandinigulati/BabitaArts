Varanasi Railway Station

Our government project at Varanasi Railway Station featured our oil paintings and metal installations, all aimed at bringing Varanasi's essence to life. Through are we showcased the local culture, city beauty, and iconic ghats. Our aim was to bring the spirit of Varanasi to life, combining tradition and spirituality.
Namami Gange Project

In a meaningful government effort, we undertook a project at the stunning ghats of Uttarkashi. Our goal was to enhance the beauty of the sacred Ganga River while vividly portraying its rich history. Through intricate murals, we captured the essence of this holy river, celebrating its cultural and spiritual significance. This project not only added to the allure of Uttarkashi's ghats but also served as a tribute to the legacy of the Ganga, leaving a lasting impression on all who visit.
